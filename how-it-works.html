About Skibidi GoFood

Built around the rhythm of campus life

Skibidi GoFood was created for MMU Melaka students who often need a faster, simpler way to get meals between lectures, meetings, and study sessions. Instead of keeping that story on a separate page, the most useful background now lives here in a shorter and more readable format.

The idea is straightforward: help students discover food quickly, place orders with less friction, and stay rewarded every time they come back.

Campus-first delivery Made for hostels, residences, labs, and common study spots around MMU Melaka.
Student-friendly payments Wallet top-up and a simple checkout flow make repeat orders quicker and easier.
Made for student routines Whether ordering solo or with friends, the flow stays simple and less crowded.
