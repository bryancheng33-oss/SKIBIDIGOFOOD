Section 1

Academic Purpose

This website, admin panel, menu mockups, reward screens, and supporting visuals are prepared solely for coursework, demonstration, prototyping, and academic evaluation.

  • No live commercial operation is intended.
  • No paid endorsement or brand partnership is implied.
  • Any resemblance to real delivery platforms is used only to communicate UX/UI concepts.

Important

This platform does not operate commercially. No real transactions occur, no payments are processed, and no actual food is delivered. All user accounts, orders, and transactions shown are for demonstration purposes only.
